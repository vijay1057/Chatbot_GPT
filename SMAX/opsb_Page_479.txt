Troubleshoot
If you fail to get the analysis view as expected, check whether the following requirements are met and modify the
configurations accordingly:
The budget line and the incurred expense lines have the same accounting period.
The cost center of the expense lines matches the cost center linked to the budget center record of the budget line.
The cost type of the expense lines matches the cost type linked to the budget type record of the budget line.
