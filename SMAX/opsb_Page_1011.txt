Activate Docker Hub account (AKS)
You need a valid Docker Hub account to download Micro Focus product images from Docker Hub.
Skip this section if you already have an active Docker Hub account that Micro Focus has authorized.
1
. 
Create a Docker Hub account at 
https://hub.docker.com
. When you enter your password for your Docker Hub account,
don't use single quotes. Make a note of your Docker Hub ID and password. 
2
. 
Request the software fulfillment and licensing team of your region to activate your Docker Hub account. Send emails to
one of these contacts (Americas: 
dockersupport.ams@microfocus.com
, Asia-Pacific: 
dockersupport.apj@microfocus.com
, and
Europe, Middle East, and Africa: 
dockersupport.emea@microfocus.com
) according to your region with the following
information:
Company name
Micro Focus customer Service Agreement ID
Docker ID
Email
License edition
You will receive an email notification from Micro Focus authorizing your Docker Hub account to access the Micro Focus
product images. This may take one or more business days. Meanwhile, you can proceed and complete the other
prerequisite tasks explained in the upcoming pages.
