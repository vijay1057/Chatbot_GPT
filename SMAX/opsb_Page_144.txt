Get started with Operations Orchestration
Operations Orchestration (OO) Containerized is a capability that enables seamless automation and orchestration of repeatable
IT operations across teams, tools, and environments.
The capability contains the following functional components:
OO Central with an Internal OO Remote Action Service (RAS)
 - These components are available when you install
OO Containerized.

life cycle.
