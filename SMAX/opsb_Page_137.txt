Get started with Service Management
The Service Management includes components as described in the following table.
Component
Description
Service
Management 
Service Management provides a tool for service desk and help desk management that quickly and
efficiently handles change and incident management. Service Management integrates a broad range of
suite capabilities, Big Data, and social collaboration to enable your workforce with connected
intelligence.
Smart
Analytics
Smart Analytics is a big data-based Service Desk solution. Smart Analytics can help you achieve the
following goals:
Improve the Help Desk management processes
Reduce the time and effort that end users and IT professionals expend on submitting requests
Accelerate the process of problem management
Improve the search experience across a variety of internal and external content
Service Portal
Service Portal enables IT departments to offer their services in an online shopping experience, similar to
the experience today at popular online retailers. With Service Portal, end users can order, track, and
manage their IT services, access knowledge articles, initiate chat conversations, and complete surveys.
Mobile app
A native mobile app that connects your organization's people and information by providing your team
access to the Service Desk applications and knowledge articles on mobile devices.
